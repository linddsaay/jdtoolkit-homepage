About Me

My name is Lindsay Keller. I am a junior at The New School in New York City where I study Journalism and Design. I am passionate about writing, literature and social justice advocacy.I grew up 45 minutes outside of NYC in southern Connecticut. There, I spent most of my time outdoors. I like to hike, bike, run at the beach and rock climb.I enjoy commuting into NYC for school because the fast pace of city life provides a nice change of environment and a different kind of inspiration for my personal writing.
